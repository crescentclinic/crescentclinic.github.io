Consultation Policy

Token:- A valid token is mandatory for each consultation. Consultation will be based on token order. Bystanders should take token separately for consultation. Both new patients and those with previous prescriptions must obtain a token. No token is required for the first follow-up visit if it occurs within one week of the previous consultation.

Emergency & Accident Cases:- We provides outpatient services only. We do not offer inpatient care, emergency care and trauma care. For emergency or trauma care, please visit a properly equipped Emergency Department or casualty. We accepts no liability for adverse outcomes caused by delays in seeking emergency care. Consultations here are conducted on a token basis, not by emergency triage.

Consultation Guidelines:- Only one bystander is allowed per patient in the consultation room or observation area. Bystander presence is mandatory for children and female patients. Bystander presence is required during suturing, surgical procedures, or drug administration. Bystander must be at least 18 years old and of sound mind. Consultation and procedure charges are fixed and non-negotiable. If unacceptable, please leave without dispute. The doctor manages both outpatients and walk-in emergency cases; delays may occur. If this is unacceptable, please seek care at another clinic or hospital.

Home Services, Telecommunication and booking:- No home services are provided. In case of an emergency outside clinic hours, visit the nearest hospital with an Emergency Department. Digital communication (SMS, email, WhatsApp) is provided for administrative convenience only. It is not a substitute for in-person medical consultation. We are not responsible for any consequences arising from delayed, missed, or misunderstood digital communication. Medical advice will not be provided via phone, social media, or messaging platforms without a formal in-person consultation. This establishment do not allows prior booking.

Conduct Inside the Clinic:- Keep phones silent. Maintain quiet and discipline; avoid shouting, arguments, or creating disturbances. Damaging clinic property will result in legal action. Photography, video or audio recording without prior permission is prohibited. Wearing a mask is mandatory inside clinic premises. Do not bring children unless they are being consulted, to minimize risk of exposure to infection. The clinic is not a place for displaying influence (muscle power, wealth, political connections) to seek priority treatment, any occasions will be dealt accordingly.

Disputes & Grievances:- Submit any complaints or grievances in writing at the reception desk, including your contact details.

Disclaimer & Limitation of Liability:- Medical advice and treatment provided at this clinic are based entirely on the information voluntarily disclosed by the patient during consultation. We are not liable for outcomes influenced by incomplete, inaccurate, or withheld information, self medication, altering treatment without consultation, non adherence to treatment or followup. Treatment results vary among individuals; no guarantees are provided or implied. While all reasonable efforts are made to minimize risks, we cannot be held liable for unforeseen adverse reactions to medications, including side effects, allergic responses, reactions related to undiagnosed medical conditions.
